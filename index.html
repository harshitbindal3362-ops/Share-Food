❗ The Problem

Every day, restaurants, bakeries, events, and households throw away fresh and edible food. At the same time, shelters, orphanages, and NGOs struggle to feed hungry people.

💡 Our Solution

ShareFood bridges the gap between food surplus and food scarcity by connecting donors with NGOs through a simple digital platform.

🍕 Donate

Food providers list surplus food in seconds.

📍 Locate

NGOs find nearby food using Google Maps.

🤝 Deliver

Food reaches the needy instead of the bin.
